Welcome to my page

My name is Jimmy Karoly. I am a graduate student at Pace University and I am completing a masters degree in computer science. This semester I am taking three core courses: Intro to parallel computing, algorithms and computing theory, and concepts and structures in internet computing. I didn't come from a computer science undergrad so I'm excited to learn all these new skills!

My favorite class so far this semester is internet computing and not just because it's the class I am doing the assignment for. I really like the class because it's teaching me practical skills that I can use to create my own projects. My other two classes are more theory based.
